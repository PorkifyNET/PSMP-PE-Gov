Imperial Leadership

The Piggian Empire is led by a dedicated group of officials committed to the prosperity and security of our great nation.

The role of Monarch is not picked democratically; If the Monarch is unfit or unable to fulfill their royal duties, all ministerial positions get shifted up.

For example: If the Monarch is unfit or unable to fulfill their royal duties, the Prime Minister becomes the new Monarch, the Minister of Defense becomes Prime Minister, etc.
